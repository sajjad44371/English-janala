Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes, it’s free to use, but you need to create an account.
3. Do I need to create an account?
Yes, you need to create a free account to use the website.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
You can build your English vocabulary by reading regularly, learning new words daily, practicing with flashcards, using the words in sentences, and engaging in conversations to strengthen memory and usage.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
No, we don’t provide certificates for completed courses.
